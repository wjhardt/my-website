About Me

I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in August 2023. My research area was additive number theory/combinatorics and my advisor was Jordan Ellenberg.

I did my undergraduate work at Carleton College , graduating with a B.A. in Mathematics in 2018.

I am currently a Ph.D. Data Science Consultant at Two Sigma Investments, where I build data science tools and machine learning models using Python and SQL to help inform investment decisions. In Summer 2022, I was a Trading Intern at Jane Street.

My current position goes until November, at which point, I will be eager to start a permanent position. I am interested, broadly, in leveraging my quantitative skills towards real-world problems.
